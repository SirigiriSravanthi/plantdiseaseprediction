About This Project

The Rice Disease Prediction System helps farmers detect diseases in rice crops using AI technology. Simply upload an image of a rice leaf, and our model will analyze it to provide a diagnosis and treatment recommendation.

Features:

  • ✔ AI-powered disease detection
  • ✔ Fast and accurate predictions
  • ✔ Easy-to-use interface
